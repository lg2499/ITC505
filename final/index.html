Revenge of Lights Out

Click a tile to toggle it and its neighbors. Turn all lights off to win.

Moves: 0
Board Size: 5 × 5

How to Play

  1. Click any square on the board.
  2. That square and its up/down/left/right neighbors will flip.
  3. Yellow = light is on, dark = light is off.
  4. Your goal is to make every tile dark.

This board is guaranteed to be solvable because the game starts from an all-off board and then applies a series of random simulated clicks using the same logic as the player. Any configuration created this way can always be undone.
