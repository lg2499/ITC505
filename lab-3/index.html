Lab 3: Serving Fonts

A web page may be viewed with the help of font installed on the computer of a visitor or with the help of some font on the server. Google fonts is one of the ways that fonts are usually served. A hosted CSS file on Google Fonts is used to refer to the font files (e.g. .woff2). On adding the connection to Google Font in our HTML <head> section, our browser will download the font on the servers of Google and use it on the text on our webpage.

The font that I have used in this case is Slackey. It is not installed on the majority of computers and connecting with it via Google Fonts, I ensure that everybody who visits this page will read the styled text in this unique and amusing font.
